How does Hitech Billsoft help manage royalties for authors?
Hitech Billsoft simplifies royalty management by allowing you to track sales per title and automatically calculate royalties based on custom agreements. This ensures accurate and timely payments to authors and agents, eliminating complex manual calculations.
Is this software suitable for small publishers without an accounting background?
Absolutely. Hitech Billsoft is designed with a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate. You don't need to be an accounting expert to manage your finances, create invoices, or track expenses, making it perfect for publishers of all sizes.

Yes, our software is fully equipped for GST compliance. It automates tax calculations, helps you create GST-compliant invoices, and generates detailed tax reports, which simplifies the entire tax filing process for your publishing business.
